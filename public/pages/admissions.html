Admission at IFER

Three phase admission process of IFER gives the applicant entry into the courses. The phases are Online Application, Written Test, and Face to Face Interview respectively. No sideways entry is entertained to be admitted at any circumstances into any of the courses but compulsory to go through each phase to be enrolled. The confirmation of admission will be sent through official mail after the admission process.

Admission Process

A structured three-step process to ensure the best candidates are selected.

Aptitude Test

Candidates go through an online aptitude test to assess their reasoning and problem-solving skills.

Group Discussion

Shortlisted candidates engage in a structured discussion to evaluate communication and leadership abilities.

Final Interview

Face-to-face interview with faculty members to assess candidates' overall suitability for the program.
